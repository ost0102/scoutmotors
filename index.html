    OUR STORY

    1961

    Before 1961, International Harvester
    built tools that helped build, feed, and
    defend America. Then they shifted
    gears, producing sport utility vehicles
    that soon found a place in American
    driveways. The grit that defined the
    first generation of tractors and tools
    for the field translated seamlessly
    into a new era. That same ruggedness
    still drives Scout Motors today.
